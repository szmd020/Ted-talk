Addictions in Animals

"Addiction is one of the most destructive forces in both human and animal lives. It starts innocently – with a small habit or curiosity – but over time, it takes hold, controlling behavior and thoughts. Whether it's a human addiction to substances or an animal seeking out harmful substances in nature, addiction can change an individual's entire existence. It's not just a physical need – it's a mental and emotional trap that is incredibly hard to escape from, leading to long-lasting damage."

Quotes

"Addiction begins with the hope that something 'out there' can instantly fill up the emptiness inside." – Jean Kilbourne

"Addiction is a way to escape reality, even if that escape is temporary." – Anonymous

"Addiction is not just a habit; it’s a way of escaping reality, and once it starts, it’s hard to live without it." – Anonymous

"Addiction is like LEGO blocks – at first, you play with them for fun, but eventually, you can’t stop, and after an hour, the whole floor is filled with pieces you don’t know how to use." – Anonymous
